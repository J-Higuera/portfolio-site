AI Movie Assistant

The AI Movie Assistant is an intelligent tool designed to help users discover movies tailored to their preferences. Enter your favorite genres, actors, or moods, and receive personalized recommendations powered by advanced AI algorithms. The assistant uses natural language processing to understand your queries and fetches movie data from online databases. It also provides trailers, ratings, and summaries to help you decide what to watch next.
